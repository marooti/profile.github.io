Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Are there regulations that protect the privacy of Zepiex's customers' data?
Absolutely! There are various laws and regulations designed to protect customer data. While it is beyond the scope of this document to delve into each regulation in detail, some notable ones include the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) that applies to the European Union and European Economic Area, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) that applies to customers and users in California, and the Privacy Act of 1988 that applies to Australian customers and users. Although these laws have similarities and differences, this FAQ will primarily focus on the GDPR.
What constitutes personal data?
In general terms, personal data refers to any information that can identify or potentially identify an individual. Examples of personal data include names, surnames, nicknames, home addresses, email addresses, telephone numbers, credit card numbers, ID numbers, geolocation data (e.g., cell phone location data), IP addresses, cookie IDs, facial and voice recognition data, among others.
What are my rights regarding my personal data?
Subject to certain limitations and restrictions, you may have specific rights concerning your personal information.
These rights may include:
  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to know what personal information we collect, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.
  • The right to rectify inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • The right to erasure or deletion ("right to be forgotten") of your personal information.
  • The right to object to our use and handling of your personal information.
  • The right to restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • The right to data portability, allowing you to transfer your personal information to another party.
  • The right to withdraw consent previously provided.
Please note that we may request additional information and verify your identity before addressing your request or determining the applicability of these rights to your specific circumstances.

Zepiex collects various types of personal data to provide exceptional workforce management software to our customers. This data may include,
but is not limited to:

How does Zepiex ensure the security of my personal data?
Zepiex employs leading technical and organizational measures to secure the physical and electronic information we store. For more details on Zepiex's security measures, please visit our Security page. Please note that no security system is completely impervious, and due to the inherent nature of the Internet, we cannot guarantee absolute safety for information during transmission or storage on our systems or in our care.
How long does Zepiex retain my personal data?
Your personal data is retained only for the period necessary for the original collection purpose, to fulfill the purposes outlined in our Privacy Policy, or to comply with legislative or regulatory obligations, such as financial reporting requirements. Once your personal data reaches the end of its retention period, we will either delete or anonymize your information. If deletion is not immediately possible, we will securely store and isolate your information from further use until deletion becomes feasible.
